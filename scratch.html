Why I Love Golf

Golf is more than just a sport to me — it's a mental challenge, a way to get outdoors, and one of the best ways I know to clear my head after a long week. Whether I'm playing a casual round with friends or competing against myself to beat my personal best, every round teaches me something new.

I've been playing since high school and love everything about it — the strategy, the precision, the patience, and the fact that no two rounds are ever the same.

My Top Golf Tips

Here are the fundamentals I focus on every time I step on the course:

  1. Master your grip
    • Neutral grip pressure — don't squeeze too tight
    • Overlap or interlocking grip — find what works for you
    • Check your grip before every shot
  2. Work on your short game
    • Chipping — practice bump-and-run shots
    • Pitching — control your distance
    • Putting — over 40% of strokes happen on the green
  3. Course management
    • Play to your strengths, not the hero shot
    • Know your distances with each club
    • Aim for the fat part of the green
  4. Mental game
    • Stay present — one shot at a time
    • Have a pre-shot routine
    • Forget bad shots quickly
